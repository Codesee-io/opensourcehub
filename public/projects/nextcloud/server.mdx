<Overview>

Why is Nextcloud so awesome?

* Access your Data: You can store your files, contacts, calendars, and more on a server of your choosing.
* Sync your Data: You keep your files, contacts, calendars, and more synchronized amongst your devices.
* Share your Data: …by giving others access to the stuff you want them to see or to collaborate with.
* Work on your Data: you can edit your documents in Nextcloud Text, Office or knowlege base. Keep notes and share them, assign tasks to team members and so on.
* A complete platform: ...with Calendar, Contacts, Mail, Video Chat and task management built right in.
* Expandable: with hundreds of apps in our App Store, you can do countless more things.
* Security: with our encryption mechanisms, HackerOne bounty program and two-factor authentication.

Do you want to learn more about how you can use Nextcloud to access, share and protect your files, calendars, contacts, communication & more at home and in your organization? Get involved? We're an open community always looking for new participants!

</Overview>
