<Overview>

Reduced.to is a modern web application that reduces the length of the link URL.
With reduced.to it's easier to remember your URLs, share, and track them.

App: https://reduced.to

We are developing reduced.to with the latest technologies available:
NestJS - Backend.
Qwik + Tailwindcss - Frontend
