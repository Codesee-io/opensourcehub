<Overview>

The Energy Autonomous Framework (EAF) by STROMDAO is an innovative open-source project designed to accelerate the integration of renewable energy sources into the modern power grid. Our mission is to empower communities, utility providers, and individuals to take control of their energy production, distribution, and consumption using modern technology stacks.

The EAF is intended for:
- Renewable energy enthusiasts and prosumers
- Developers and innovators in the energy sector
- Utility providers looking to adopt smart grid technologies
- Policy makers aiming to support renewable energy initiatives

</Overview>
